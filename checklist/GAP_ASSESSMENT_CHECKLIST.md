## SCORING & PRIORITISATION

### Score Your Checklist:
- **Critical** (❌ Not Done + Penalty > ₹100 Cr): Fix in 30 days
- **High** (❌ Not Done + Penalty ₹50–100 Cr): Fix in 60 days
- **Medium** (⚠️ Partial + Any penalty): Fix in 90 days
- **Low** (Best practice items): Plan for 6 months

### Quick Win Summary (items that can be done in < 2 weeks):
- [ ] Update Privacy Policy
- [ ] Add Board complaint reference to consent notices (Rule 3)
- [ ] Designate a Privacy Lead or DPO (even informally)
- [ ] Document what data you collect and why (basic data inventory)
- [ ] Add breach notification SLA clause to top 3 vendor contracts

### 5. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER DPDP ACT 2023

You have the following rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023:

**a) Right to Information (Section 11)**  
You can ask us to confirm whether we are processing your personal data and
obtain a summary of the data we hold about you.

**b) Right to Correction (Section 12)**  
You can ask us to correct inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated personal data.

**c) Right to Erasure (Section 12)**  
You can ask us to delete your personal data. We will comply unless we are
required to retain it by law (e.g., regulatory requirements).

**d) Right to Grievance Redressal (Section 13)**  
If you are unsatisfied with how we handle your personal data, you can raise
a grievance with us (see Section 7 below).

**e) Right to Nominate (Section 14)**  
You can nominate another individual to exercise these rights on your behalf
in the event of your death or incapacity.

**f) Right to Withdraw Consent (Section 6(4))**  
You can withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawing consent will not
affect the lawfulness of processing before the withdrawal.

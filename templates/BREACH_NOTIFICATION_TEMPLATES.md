# Personal Data Breach Notification Templates
## Rule 7 of DPDP Rules 2025 — Schedule 3 Format

> Two templates: (A) Board Notification | (B) Data Principal Notification
> Both must be sent "as soon as reasonably practicable" after breach discovery.
